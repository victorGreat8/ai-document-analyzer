Quarterly Business Review - Q1 2025
Acme Corporation | Confidential

Executive Summary
Acme Corporation delivered strong results in Q1 2025, with total revenue reaching $4.2 million,
representing a 18% year-over-year growth. Customer acquisition improved significantly, adding
320 new enterprise clients. However, operating costs increased by 12% due to infrastructure
expansion, which slightly compressed margins.

Key Highlights
- Launched new product line "AcmeCloud Pro" in February, already accounting for 15% of revenue
- Expanded into 3 new markets: Brazil, Germany, and South Korea
- Customer satisfaction score improved from 82 to 91 out of 100
- Engineering headcount grew from 45 to 67 employees

Challenges
The company faced supply chain delays in hardware components, impacting delivery timelines by
an average of 3 weeks. Competitor pricing pressure in the SMB segment led to a 5% churn increase
in that customer tier.

Outlook
Q2 2025 guidance targets $4.8 million in revenue. The leadership team is confident that the
new product line and international expansion will offset competitive pressures. A cost optimization
initiative is underway to bring operating margins back to 28% by end of Q2.
